## Full description — EN
Breaktime Rebellion is a retro pixel-art arcade game set in an Italian high school of the '80s and '90s. Dodge the teachers, complete your missions and cause chaos: it's the most rebellious school year in the history of Liceo Manzoni.

You are Marco, and you have a mission for every floor, every corridor, every break.
Tag the chalkboards with spray paint, deflate the gym ball, flood the bathroom, plant firecrackers in the bins — and try not to get caught by the patrolling teachers.

10 levels. 10 missions. One legendary school year.

🏫 3-floor school with '80s/'90s pixel-art arcade visuals
🕹️ Stealth gameplay: teachers, janitor and principal with patrol AI and line-of-sight
🌙 Level 10: night mode, guards, escape with the class register
🎮 Floating analog joystick, built for touch
🎵 Retro 8-bit music and SFX
🏆 3 difficulty levels with progressive unlock
🌍 English and Italian
📴 Free, no ads, playable offline
