Privacy Policy — Breaktime Rebellion

Last updated: May 2026

1. No data collected

Breaktime Rebellion does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app does not require an account, does not use analytics, and does not contact any external server.

2. Local storage

Game progress (difficulty setting, unlocked levels) is saved locally on your device using the browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

3. Permissions

The app does not request any device permissions (camera, microphone, location, contacts, etc.).

4. Third-party services

The app does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics services.

5. Children

The app does not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue on GitHub.
